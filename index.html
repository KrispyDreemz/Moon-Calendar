Respect & care

This app is inspired by Indigenous calendrical concepts and well-known full moon names. It is not a replacement for any cultural tradition or an authoritative source of Indigenous knowledge.

The archetypes and affirmations offered here are meant to be grounding and non-clinical, inviting reflection rather than prediction.
